Welcome to Stephanie Kothdiwala Photography

BEHIND THE LENS

(Click on the play button at the bottom of the page to listen to my welcome message.)

I'm not just a photographer, I'm a creative visionary on a mission to capture the extraordinary in every moment. From the tender embrace of a newlywed couple to the vibrant energy of a bustling event, I thrive on immortalizing life's most precious moments through my lens.

With a passion that knows no bounds, my photography services transcend the ordinary, encompassing a diverse range of subjects. Whether it's portraiture, weddings, family or product, I approach each project with enthusiasm and dedication.

My journey into the world of photography began as a simple curiosity, but it quickly evolved into a lifelong passion. In 2012, I embarked on a transformative journey when I enrolled in a Digital Photography course at Barbados Community College. Little did I know that this decision would ignite the spark of my professional career.

Since then, I've had the privilege of capturing countless love stories, immortalizing cherished memories, and leaving a lasting impact on everyone I photograph. My dedication to the craft has been recognized time and time again, earning me accolades such as the top spot in the inaugural Photography competition at Barbados Community College and triumph in the prestigious SOL 2013 calendar photography contest.

As I continue on this exhilarating journey, I am fueled by a relentless drive to push boundaries, unleash creativity, and deliver unparalleled excellence with every click of my camera. Join me as we embark on a visual adventure, where each image tells a unique story and every moment is a masterpiece in the making.
